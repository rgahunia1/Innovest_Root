Who we are ....

This capstone project centers on developing an AI-powered loan approval model specifically tailored for the Southern Ontario Fund for Investment in Innovation (SOFII). The research that supports this project, seeks to evolve SOFII's current loan approval procedures by incorporating both financial and non-financial metrics into a cohesive model. This approach aims to improve decision-making precision, mitigate biases, and streamline the overall approval process, with a focus on catering to the needs of high-growth small-to-medium sized enterprises (SMEs) in the early scale-up phase across Southeastern Ontario.
